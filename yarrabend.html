YarraBend Amphitheatre
Description

A project I completed very early on, where the aim was to make a proposal to fit in an amphitheatre into the natural hill at YarraBend Park. The first step was surveying the area, by getting spot heights, locations of fences, paths, and trees. This was all then put into a CAD software which read our readings and output a rough map of the area. We were then tasked to input additional paths and of course the amphitheatre and import it into ArcScene.

In ArcScene we were able to symbolise/design the area a bit more by changing the design of the trees, paths and the amphitheatre. Below is the ArcSene display before symbolic changes were made, and the final proposal map of the area, with new proposed paths and amphitheatre. Improvements could definitely be made and over the next 3 years I have been able to improve my projects.
