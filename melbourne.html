Road accident hotspots, City vs Suburb
Description

This projectâ€™s main goal was to compare the road accident hotspots of a main city with a suburb, to see the difference between the hotspots and their patterns. The city was Melbourne CBD, and the suburb was Dandenong and as you can see there are quite major differences between the two locations.

Melbourne CBD has hotspots all over the network which change depending on the time of day, Morning peak hour (7-9am) and Evening peak hour (4-7pm). On the other hand Dandenong has a very strong hotspot around the town centre that stays throughout the day only slightly changing in intensities.
