Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology
Bachelor of Science (Geospatial Science) (Honours), from March 2018 - October 2021
Australia, Melbourne

RMIT's bachelor of Geospatial science is revered amongst all of Australia and slowly and steadily the world. It has lots of connections within the industry and helped me get a very solid grasp of spatial and data analysis. I graduated with a WAM of 71 and a GPA of 3.0.

Specializations
  • The course offers a wide range of avenues from surveying to photogrammetry, to Remote sensing and more. My main focus and interest though was with GIS, (Geographic Information System) which focuses on spatial and data analysis
  • In a general sense I have had great success in being able to find data, analyse what it means, cut it down to what I need and then use it for a variety of purposes, such as road and hotspot analysis, or map making.
  • Through GIS I was very interested in road accidents and Victoria’s goal of towards 0. Through this interest I was able to analyse car crashes and hotspots through three major projects were I created and analysed road accident hotspots and their patterns.

Key achievements
  • My main achievement would be my work. Being able to produce high quality analysis and then being able to produce a product that shows the analyse is something I'm very proud of. Being able to explain what I did and how I did it, is also a key achievement as well, as explaining something so someone who doesn't understand the project/work is something I'm always trying to improve on.
  • I'm constantly trying to improve my social skills, and through the course I have been able to make strong progress on this. I have been able to meet new people and work within groups, writing joint reports or a group presentation. Being able to do this has strengthen my social and public skills and I hope to continue to do so wherever my career takes me.
  • An achievement more focused on grades would be that I scored very well in my spatial analysis classes. I was able to achieve distinctions in all the courses with one High Distinction in the last course based on spatial analysis.
