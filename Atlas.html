Calendar Atlas project
Description

This was a group project consisting of 12 members with the aim of making a calendar atlas. The maps focused on towns that were heavily hit in the 2019-2020 Australian bushfire season and hoped to encourage local and international tourists to visit this towns to help get the towns back on their feet. As a group we did some research on the multitude of towns and selected 12 towns we thought were interesting and each picked one to map. I selected Batemans Bay which was probably the biggest town and most urban town out of all 12 which provided some challenges.

As this project took place during 2020, covid hindered the progress we were able to make as instead of working by ourselves and meeting up once a week to check progress and work as a team, we were forced online were working together became a bit of a struggle. Despite this though within 4 months, we were able to work together and produce 12 similar looking maps to form a coherent calendar. My contribution can be seen below.
